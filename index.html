How to Play

Find every listed word hidden in the grid. Words can run in any of the 8 directions (horizontal, vertical, diagonal — forwards or backwards), but always in a straight line.

Tap a letter to start, then tap the next letter in line to extend your word — each tap adds a numbered badge showing the order you picked it in.

Tap the last letter you picked again to undo it. Tap an unrelated letter to start a new word from there instead.

Two words can cross through the same letter. As soon as your selection spells a listed word, it's marked found automatically.

Use the ⋮ menu to change the word category, difficulty (grid size), and language at any time.
